Markets We Serve

+

AutoVerse supplies precision components to customers across five core market verticals, supported by dedicated application engineering teams for each segment. Our customer list includes Maruti Suzuki, Tata Motors, Mahindra, Bajaj Auto, Hero MotoCorp, Ashok Leyland, and international OEMs including Stellantis, Volkswagen Group, and Daimler Trucks.

  • Passenger Vehicles — compact cars to premium SUVs, petrol, diesel, and hybrid
  • Commercial Vehicles — LCVs, HCVs, buses, and specialty fleet vehicles
  • Two-Wheelers — entry motorcycles to performance bikes and e-scooters
  • Off-Highway — construction, agriculture, material handling
  • Electric Vehicles — motor housings, battery enclosures, thermal components
  • Export Markets — Germany, Japan, USA, Mexico, Thailand, South Africa

Automotive Components

+

Our core manufacturing capability covers the powertrain, chassis, and body-in-white systems. Each component family has a dedicated production cell with specialized tooling, fixtures, and quality gates calibrated to the tightest drawing tolerances.

Engine & Powertrain:

  • Cylinder heads & blocks (aluminium and grey iron)
  • Connecting rods and crankshaft journal bearings
  • Camshaft covers and valve train components
  • Oil pump housings and filter adapters
  • Turbocharger housings and heat shields
  • Transmission cases and shift forks

Chassis & Suspension:

  • Control arms and steering knuckles
  • Brake caliper housings and anchor plates
  • Wheel hubs and bearing flanges
  • Subframe brackets and cross-members

OEM Products

+

Our OEM business accounts for 70% of revenue. We are a preferred Tier 1 and Tier 2 supplier to India's largest vehicle manufacturers, operating under long-term supply agreements with annual volume commitments. All OEM products are manufactured to the customer's proprietary drawings with full PPAP documentation.

  • Program management from DFMEA to PPAP Level 3
  • Co-located engineering support at customer facilities on request
  • EDI integration with customer ERP systems (SAP, Oracle)
  • JIT delivery directly to assembly line — no buffer warehousing
  • Kanban replenishment with 48-hour emergency pull capability
  • Dedicated quality engineer resident at top-3 customer plants
  • Annual cost-reduction proposals (VA/VE) submitted every Q3
  • Digital twin capability for virtual prototype validation

Aftermarket Products

+

AutoVerse's aftermarket division — operating under the AV Pro brand — offers a catalogue of 2,400+ part numbers covering vehicles up to 12 years in service. Our aftermarket parts match or exceed OEM dimensional and material specifications, and are distributed through a network of 1,800+ authorised dealers across India.

  • Brake pads, rotors, and caliper rebuild kits
  • Engine gasket sets and head gaskets
  • Timing belt and chain kits with tensioners
  • Wheel bearing hub assemblies
  • Clutch pressure plate and disc kits
  • Fuel pump and injector service kits
  • Air filter and oil filter elements (OEM-equivalent)
  • Anti-roll bar links and rubber bushings
  • Shock absorber mounts and bump stops
  • Wiper mechanisms and motor assemblies

Quality Standards

+

Quality is not a department at AutoVerse — it is a built-in attribute of every process. We operate a zero-defect manufacturing philosophy anchored by our 7-gate Quality Management System, which controls every stage from raw material receipt through to customer shipment.

  • IATF 16949:2016 certified across all three plants
  • ISO 9001:2015 — baseline QMS for all product families
  • ISO 14001:2015 — environmental management at each site
  • OHSAS 18001 — occupational health and safety system
  • 100% critical dimension inspection via Zeiss CMM
  • Real-time SPC on all key process characteristics
  • Automated vision inspection at end-of-line for cosmetic attributes
  • Cp/Cpk ≥ 1.67 required at all critical features before production release
  • Annual internal audits + bi-annual third-party surveillance
  • Customer-specific requirements (CSR) management per AIAG/VDA
