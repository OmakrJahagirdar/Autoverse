OEM Supply

OEM Products

We manufacture to exact OEM specifications, working directly with vehicle manufacturers from design validation to mass production.

Our engineering team works alongside OEM design teams from concept through PPAP. We provide DFM analysis, prototype fabrication, and full APQP documentation.

Dedicated production lines with SPC monitoring, automated inspection, and 100% traceability for volumes from 10,000 to 10 million parts annually.

98.7% on-time delivery backed by regional warehousing, dedicated logistics partnerships, and real-time supply chain visibility tools.

OEM-equivalent aftermarket parts for the global replacement market, distributed through 50+ country distributor networks with full warranty support.
