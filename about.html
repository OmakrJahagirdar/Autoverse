Our Story

A Legacy of Manufacturing Excellence

Founded in 1989 in Pune — India's automotive capital — AutoVerse began as a small precision machining workshop. Today we operate three facilities spanning 280,000 sq. ft., serving 200+ OEM partners across 50 countries.

Our vertically integrated model — from raw material procurement to finished component delivery — gives clients unmatched quality control, cost efficiency, and supply chain reliability.

Our Journey

35 Years of Growth

From a 5-man workshop in Pimpri to a global precision manufacturer — here's how we grew.

1989
Founded in Pune

Started as a 5-person precision machining workshop in Pimpri-Chinchwad.

1997
First OEM Partnership

Secured first Tier-1 supply agreement with Tata Motors for engine components.

2004
ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 quality certification and expanded to 500 employees.

2011
Chennai Plant Opened

Opened Plant 2 in Chennai, adding dedicated stamping and body-in-white capabilities.

2018
IATF 16949 & Export Growth

Achieved IATF 16949 certification and began exporting to 25+ countries.

2024
2,000+ Employees & EV Pivot

Launched EV-specific component lines and crossed 2,000 employees milestone.
