Who We Are

Driving Industry Forward with Precision

AutoVerse is a global-scale automotive components manufacturer headquartered in Pune, India. For over three decades, we have supplied precision-engineered parts to the world's foremost vehicle manufacturers, combining metallurgical expertise with cutting-edge automation.

Our vertically integrated facilities span 280,000 sq. ft. of production floor, housing 140+ CNC machining centers, robotic welding stations, and real-time quality inspection systems—ensuring every component that leaves our dock is manufactured to the highest tolerances in the industry.

Why AutoVerse

The AutoVerse Advantage

Six pillars that define why global manufacturers choose AutoVerse as their long-term precision parts partner.

01

Micron-Level Precision

Our 5-axis CNC centers and coordinate measuring machines achieve tolerances down to ±0.005mm consistently across high-volume production runs.

02

Rapid Prototyping

From CAD file to physical prototype in under 72 hours using our in-house 3D printing and rapid tooling capabilities.

03

Integrated Quality Control

Every part is 100% inspected using optical CMM, vision systems, and Zeiss measurement technology before leaving our facility.

04

Global Supply Chain

Our logistics network spans 50+ countries with regional warehousing in Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Americas for just-in-time delivery.

05

Sustainable Manufacturing

ISO 14001 certified environmental management. 40% of our energy comes from renewable sources. Zero liquid discharge facilities.

06

Dedicated Engineering Support

A 120-person R&D team provides DFM consulting, material selection, and continuous improvement support throughout the product lifecycle.
