Why Choose Us

+

AutoVerse is more than just a job — it is a career platform designed for forward-thinking engineering minds. We are India's fastest-growing Tier 1 automotive supplier, consistently adding new production capabilities and expanding into premium international EV and hybrid markets. Working here puts you at the absolute frontier of manufacturing technology, where you will operate state-of-the-art multi-axis CNC machines, advanced collaborative robotics (cobots), and real-time Industry 4.0 IoT analytics systems.

  • Global Scale: Work on critical components going into passenger electric vehicles, luxury sedans, and heavy transport fleets sold in 50+ countries.
  • Direct Client Interface: Get direct engineering exposure by collaborating directly with Tier-1 international OEM design teams.
  • State-of-the-Art Design Stack: Utilize premium modeling, simulation, and FEA tools (CATIA, ANSYS, Siemens NX) in our advanced R&D Innovation Labs.
  • Structured Career Progression: Advance through a transparent grading structure with twice-yearly skill audits and clear pathing criteria.
  • Market-Leading Compensation: Receive highly competitive salaries benchmarked against the top-quartile of the automotive sector, plus annual performance bonuses.
  • Equity Sharing: Participate in ESOP programs for key technical leads, engineering specialists, and management roles.
  • Safe & Premium Environment: Work in clean, air-conditioned, and ergonomically optimized facilities with a 100% commitment to zero-accident safety protocols.
  • Leadership & Strategy: Connect directly with executive leadership through quarterly town halls and direct mentorship channels.
  • Grassroots Innovation: Pitch ideas to the AutoVerse Innovation Board — with implemented improvements earning recognition and awards up to ₹50,000.

Employee Benefits

+

We believe that competitive benefits are the foundation of employee retention and well-being. AutoVerse's benefits package is benchmarked annually against the top-quartile of automotive suppliers in India.

  • Mediclaim policy — ₹5 lakh family floater, zero co-pay at network hospitals
  • Term life insurance — 5× annual CTC for all permanent employees
  • Personal accident cover — ₹10 lakh per employee
  • Provident Fund — 12% employer contribution beyond statutory
  • Gratuity — full statutory entitlement, funded through approved trust
  • Subsidised meals at all plant and office canteens
  • Company transport from 14 pick-up points across Pune, Chennai, Manesar
  • 15 days earned leave + 12 casual/sick days + 12 paid public holidays
  • Maternity leave — 26 weeks fully paid; paternity leave — 10 days
  • Creche facility and childcare allowance at Pune headquarters

Learning & Development

+

AutoVerse invests ₹2,200 per employee per year in training — more than 3× the automotive industry average. Our AutoVerse Academy is a dedicated learning function with full-time L&D professionals who design, source, and deliver programmes across technical, functional, and leadership tracks.

  • AutoVerse Academy — online + in-person learning platform with 400+ modules
  • GD&T and APQP certification programmes (AIAG-approved curriculum)
  • Six Sigma Green Belt sponsorship for production engineers
  • Annual deputation to SIAM, CII, and ACMA industry conferences
  • Mentoring programme — every new hire assigned a senior buddy for 6 months
  • Management fast-track for high-potential performers (top 10% cohort)
  • International exchange — 3-month secondments to customer plants in Europe/Japan
  • MBA sponsorship for mid-career professionals (part-time/weekend programmes)
  • Tuition reimbursement for job-relevant courses up to ₹40,000/year

Company Culture

+

Culture at AutoVerse is built on four values we call the AutoVerse Code: Precision, Integrity, Innovation, and People First. These are not slogans on a poster — they are the criteria by which we hire, promote, and recognise our people.

Our plants operate under a flat hierarchy compared to industry peers. Every technician on the shopfloor has direct access to the plant manager through our open-door programme. Monthly all-hands meetings at each plant keep everyone aligned on targets, challenges, and wins.

  • Annual AutoVerse Excellence Awards — 12 categories, 36 winners plant-wide
  • Shopfloor innovation boards — kaizen ideas visible to all, credited to their authors
  • Sports & wellness programme — cricket, football, badminton leagues
  • AutoVerse Cares CSR programme — employees contribute 2 hours/month to community
  • Diversity targets — 30% women in technical roles by 2027
  • Mental wellness helpline (24×7) for all employees and immediate family
  • Quarterly skip-level conversations — every employee meets their +2 annually
  • Pulse survey every 6 months with published action plans within 30 days
